Privacy Policy ANALYSER

This project is made in HackDoomna Hackathon during the Invicta'20 Fest. The focus of this web application is to summarise any privacy policy that you can provide. It is understood that the policy is very lengthy. In our daily life we just press yes in every privacy policy we see, without even knowing the contents, trusting the company or the application blindly. In this age of information we should be capable enough to understand what we are agreeing to without needing much effort.
